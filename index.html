About Us:

Finding the best laptop for your money can feel like an endless search. There are so many quality laptops on the market that choosing a suitable one at a great price takes a lot of effort. That’s why at FLC, we organize our laptops into categories, breaking down the best laptop for each purpose and lifestyle.

We want to help you find a laptop that feels tailored to your needs. Our laptops are customized based on work, entertainment, education, gaming, and everyday use. We design laptops with user satisfaction in mind, at a price everyone can love, including:

  • High-performance laptops for creators, professionals, or gamers – we’ve got powerhouses that rival what desktops deliver.
  • Ultraportable, thin and light laptops for folks who are always on the go.
  • Budget laptops that give you value and performance in a single package.
  • Web-based Chromebook laptops – great for students or anyone seeking a cheaper price tag for more lightweight computing.
  • Innovative 2 in 1s – part laptop, part tablet, part nothing you’ve seen before – for those who want to tap the untested power of the new.

The transformative 2 in 1 laptop:

For the same features and low cost, but with an innovative design, check out our 2 in 1 laptops like Lenovo Yoga and Flex. These cutting-edge PCs easily convert from laptop to tablet and back. You can enjoy the touchscreen functionality of a tablet, while still taking advantage of the speed and versatility of a laptop with touchscreen features, 4K display options, and more.

Gaming laptops: PC gaming beyond your desk:

Gamers don’t have to feel bound to their desktops anymore. A gaming laptop from the Lenovo Legion or IdeaPad Gaming line will let you dominate your competitors wherever you are. Are you hardcore? We’ve got you covered with customizable models that boast powerful graphics, VR-ready options, and overclockable CPUs. We’ve also got budget options for casual gamers. Whatever laptop you choose to game on, be prepared to lose yourself in immersive audio and vivid displays.

For work and businesses:

For the powerhouse of our laptop lines, check out the legendary Lenovo ThinkPad. This includes the ThinkPad P Series -- the most robust, high-performance workstations we've ever created. They’re outfitted with stellar graphics, terabytes of storage, and a potent amount of memory. Certified by independent software vendors (ISVs), these laptops are recognized worldwide as an industry workhorse. For professionals that need to work fast and effectively, but affordability is a must, the ThinkBook is designed with ThinkPad-like features at a price point perfect for small- to medium-sized businesses.
